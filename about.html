About Fishing Tribune

Fishing Tribune is an independent fishing gear review site built by anglers, for anglers. We test, compare, and honestly review fishing equipment so you can spend less time researching and more time on the water.

Our Mission

The fishing gear market is flooded with paid placements and fake reviews. We started Fishing Tribune because we were tired of buying gear based on hype, only to be disappointed on the water. Every review on this site is based on real specifications, real user feedback, and real-world performance data.

How We Review

For every product category, we analyze specifications, cross-reference user reviews from multiple sources, compare pricing across retailers, and assess long-term durability. When possible, we test products ourselves. When we can't, we rely on verified purchaser feedback and expert angler opinions.

How We Make Money

Fishing Tribune is reader-supported. When you click our links and make a purchase, we may earn a small affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. This revenue keeps the site running and lets us continue producing honest reviews. No brand pays for placement. No manufacturer sponsors our rankings. See our full affiliate disclosure for details.

Our Team

Our editorial team combines 50+ years of angling experience across freshwater bass fishing, saltwater fly fishing, kayak fishing, and tournament competition. We fish rivers, lakes, oceans, and everything in between.
