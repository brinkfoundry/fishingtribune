About Fishing Tribune

Why We Exist

The fishing gear market is flooded with paid placements and fake reviews. We started Fishing Tribune because we were tired of buying gear based on hype. Every review is based on real specs, real user feedback, and real-world performance.

Our Methodology

  • Market analysis — 50+ products per category narrowed to genuine contenders
  • Spec comparison — manufacturer data and independent measurements
  • User review aggregation — Amazon, Bass Pro, forums, YouTube cross-referenced
  • Expert consultation — tournament anglers, guides, tackle shop owners
  • Value assessment — performance per dollar, not just absolute quality

Editorial Standards

No brand pays for placement. No manufacturer sponsors our rankings. If a product is bad, we say so.

Our Team

50+ years of combined angling experience — freshwater bass, saltwater fly, kayak fishing, ice fishing, tournament competition.

How We Make Money

Reader-supported via Amazon affiliate commissions at no extra cost to you. See our full disclosure.
