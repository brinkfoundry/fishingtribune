FAQ

3. What is the difference between CHIRP and traditional sonar?

CHIRP (Compressed High-Intensity Radar Pulse) sonar provides more detailed and accurate information about the underwater environment, including structure and fish. Traditional sonar uses a single frequency to detect fish and structure.

FAQ

4. Can I use a carp fish finder in saltwater?

Most carp fish finders are designed for freshwater use, but some models, like the Garmin EchoMAP 42cv, can be used in saltwater. Be sure to check the manufacturer's specifications before making a purchase.
