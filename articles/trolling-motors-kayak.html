What matters in a kayak trolling motor

  • Thrust: Power to move you and gear without struggle on current days. Consider weight and gear load.
  • Shaft length: Long enough to keep prop in clean water, avoid cavitation when the bow rides chop.
  • Battery and runtime: Capacity to last a day on the water; plan for extra run time if fishing.
  • Mount type: Hinge vs shaft lock mounts, quick-release features.
